History

  • 1977Established Kuo Chie Enterprise Co., Ltd. at Xinzhuang, Taipei County and produced Christmas tree light copper slice electrical switch.
  • 1983Moved to Pate, Taoyuan County and renamed Kuo Chie Precision Industrial Co., Ltd.
  • 1985Transferred the production to AC copper plates, terminals, socket leaf spring, electrical machinery components.
  • 1995Single and double terminal type products approved by UL.
  • 2001Established Dongguan Smart Metal Plating Co., Ltd. in China.
  • 2004/01Company transfer and Director/Supervisor change.
  • 2004/03Kuo Chie Precision Industrial Co., Ltd. passed ISO9001/2000 Approval.
  • 2005/01Combined Chun Yuan Industrial Co., Ltd. (Chung Fu Factory) and added injection molding products.
  • 2005/07Dongguan Smart Metal Plating Co., Ltd. passed ISO9001/2000 Approval.
  • 2007Established NanJing KuoChang Electronic Science & Technology Co., Ltd. in China.
  • 2007/02Added the injection molding equipments (2 injection machines, 1 mixer, 3 material dryers, 1 microwave machine).
  • 2007/09Obtained the trademark registration of KC written characters from Kuo Chie Precision Industrial Co., Ltd.
  • 2008/03Products obtained the patent right.
  • 2009/07Passed ISO9001/2008 Approval alteration.
  • 2009/08Registration of NJKC trademark in China.
  • 2010/07NanJing KuoChang Electronic Science & Technology Co., Ltd. passed ISO14001/2004 Approval.
  • 2010/07/11Dongguan Smart Metal Plating Co., Ltd. passed ISO14001/2004 Approval.
